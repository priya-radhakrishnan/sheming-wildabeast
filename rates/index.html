How we calculate your rate estimates

From your usage
We base our estimates on the way you currently use energy. We used your hourly usage from the last 3 months to estimate your annual usage for each rate.

These estimates will be more accurate when you have 12 full months of history, and may change significantly if we have your usage for both summer and winter. Also, please keep in mind that these values only reflect past usage. Your future costs may be different.

Why are we offering these rates?

We’re offering these rates to help you lower your bills and to provide you with better service.

These rates will:
Put you in control of your costs: If you can reduce your use during certain times you can save significantly. We want you to be able to pick the rate that best meets your needs.

Provide more reliable service: These rates help reduce the use of electricity in times of high demand, reducing the possibility of brownouts.

Reduce the need for more power plants: These rates are structured to better distribute energy use, so we can utilize our current power plants more effectively.
