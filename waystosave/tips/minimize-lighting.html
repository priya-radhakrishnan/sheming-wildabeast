Minimize use of lighting

An average home’s lighting bill can account for up to 20% of electricity costs. Turning lights off when you’re not in the room is a simple way for you to make a dent in your lighting costs.

Things to think about:

  • Make it a habit to turn lights off each time you leave a room.
  • Before you go to sleep, walk through your home to shut off any forgotten lights.
  • Encourage other family members to turn off their lights as well. This is a great way for young children to do their part in saving electricity.
