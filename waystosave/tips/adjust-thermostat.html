Program your thermostat

Why? Setting your temperature about 10 degrees lower for about 8 hours a day can result in significant savings â€” up to 10% of your heating bills.

Step-by-step

  • During colder months, lower the thermostat before leaving your home. During warmer months, raise the thermostat before leaving your home.
  • Before you go to sleep, turn down the thermostat in colder months and turn up the thermostat in warmer months. Use an extra blanket to stay warm in colder months, or a fan to stay cool in warmer months.
  • Only heat or cool the zones you occupy: If you have separate thermostats on different floors or in different areas, adjust the thermostat for energy savings in the area you are not using.
  • Program it: Consider installing a programmable thermostat.
